5	ZKM is THE museum for new media art in the world. http://on1.zkm.de/zkm/e/It shows interactive art, video art, sculpture, paintings... engaging with the latest in technology. It's huge... includes the Museum of Contemporary Art, the Media Museum, the Institute for Music and Accoustics. In the Media Museum, one can see interactive films, simulation techniques for cyberspace, experiments in video gaming. The shows are ambitious and well-curated, i.e. FUN.ZKM's one of its kind.  Go visit the next time you're in Germany. :-)
